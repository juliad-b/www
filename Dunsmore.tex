\subsection{Dunsmore}

\uppercase{FIRST GENERATION]

The first person in this line that we know of is \uppercase{Robert Dunsmore), who was born in 1780 in North Ayrshire.  By	
17 April 1808 he was living at Craighouse, 	Old Cumnock, Ayrshire, and had married \uppercase{Jean Kirkland}, 1777 - 1832.  They had five children: James Dunsmuir, Marian Dunsmuir, Allan Dunsmuir, Mary Dunsmore, and \uppercase{Jean Dunsmore} who was \uppercase{Joan Nyria Hancox}'s great grandmother.
